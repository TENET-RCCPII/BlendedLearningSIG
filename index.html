eLearning/Blended Learning Special Interest Group

A website where opportunities, challenges and knowledge about eLearning/Blended learning in the South African Higher Education sector is shared.

Learn more

What is the eLearning/Blended Learning SIG?

In 2017 the Special Interest Group was established by members of the Association for South African Universities Directors of Information Technology (ASAUDIT) to create opportunities for academics, IT staff, and teaching and learning practitioners to share experience, challenges, knowledge, and resources across institutional and departmental boundaries.

Share Knowledge and Lessons Learned

eLearning and blended learning practitionars and technology providers can share anecdotes and information in and informal way via the blog.

Find Expertise

A database with information relevant to specific practices, standards, technologies in use at universities in South Africa will be developed.

Training Resources

Training resources will be deveped collaboratively and published under open licenses to facilitate re-use and remix.
